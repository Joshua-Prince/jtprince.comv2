Maximum Tower



Instructions: Click in the grass to place a tower (-300 power). Click an existing tower to upgrade it (-800 power for tier 2, -1800 power for tier 3). Win by reaching 10,000 power before 5 attackers reach your castle!

Created by Will Chilcote, Joshua Prince, and Jason Zhang in 12 hours as part of UC Davis Game Jam, Fall 2016
Special thanks to Kristin Kirk and Jiad Asadi for sound effects
