Joshua T. Prince

Computer Science and Engineering Undergraduate
University of California, Davis


My name is Joshua Prince, and I love tinkering with anything involving computers. I found an interest in programming as early as middle school, and taught myself the Java programming language during high school to modify and create simple games and desktop apps. I entered the field of Computer Science and Engineering to more formally learn how to solve problems with the help of the computer. It is my dream to use my skills as a computer scientist to advance the world's perception of technology.
